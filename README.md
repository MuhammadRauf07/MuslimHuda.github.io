Last Updated: February 2026

MuslimHuda ("we," "our," or "us") operates the MuslimHuda mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the App.

By accessing or using MuslimHuda, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

MuslimHuda does not require user registration and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number.

However, the App may collect certain information as described below.

A. Location Information

The App requests access to your device location to provide accurate prayer times based on your geographic region.

Location access is used solely to calculate prayer times.

We do not store your precise location on our servers.

Location data is processed only as necessary to provide prayer time functionality.

If location permission is denied, prayer time features may not function accurately. Other features of the App will remain available.

You may enable or disable location permissions at any time through your device settings.

B. Usage and Analytics Information

We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized usage data to improve app performance and user experience. This may include:

App interactions

Screens viewed

Session duration

Device information

General geographic region (country-level)

This data does not personally identify users.

Google Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

C. Crash and Diagnostic Information

We use Firebase Crashlytics to monitor app stability and identify technical issues. Crash reports may include:

Device model

Operating system version

App version

Technical diagnostic information

This information is used solely to improve the reliability and performance of the App.

D. Push Notifications

MuslimHuda uses Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send push notifications, including prayer reminders, Ramadan alerts, daily Duas, and important announcements.

To deliver notifications, a unique device token may be generated. This token does not directly identify you and is used only for notification delivery.

Push notifications are optional. You may disable them at any time through your device settings.
